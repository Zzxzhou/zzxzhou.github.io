Research

Panel Quantile Regression with Heterogeneous Slope Coefficients (Work in progress)
with Yutao Sun · Nov 2023 – Present
Working paper coming soon

We develop a smoothed panel quantile regression framework with heterogeneous slopes and multi-way fixed effects. Using Conquer-style convolution smoothing with higher-order kernels and data-driven bandwidths, we derive analytical incidental-parameter bias corrections for fixed effects and construct inference procedures that remain valid in panels.
